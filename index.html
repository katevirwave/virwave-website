What is VirWave?

A breathing and wellness companion app that helps you find calm, focus, and clarity — anytime, anywhere.

🌊

Guided Breathing

Choose your breathing shape, rhythm, and color mood. Breathe along with a beautiful, responsive visual guide that adapts to you.

📓

Personal Journal

Reflect on your practice with mood tracking, tags, and private entries. Your thoughts stay yours — always.

🤝

Connections

Share your calm with someone who matters. Connect with friends and family through a simple, private share code.

Built for every kind of mind

Whether you're neurotypical or neurodivergent, a seasoned meditator or a total beginner — VirWave meets you where you are.

Sensory-safe by design

No jarring animations, no sensory overload. Every visual and interaction is designed to be calming first. Motion sensitivity? We've got reduced-motion support built in.

Your pace, your way

Customize everything — breathing speed, visual style, session length. VirWave adapts to your preferences, not the other way around. No pressure, no streaks, no guilt.

Millennials & Gen Z

Designed for the generation navigating burnout, doom-scrolling, and the pursuit of calm in a noisy world. Premium feel without the pretension.

Inclusive language

We don't assume what calm looks like for you. VirWave uses welcoming, non-prescriptive language throughout — because wellness isn't one-size-fits-all.

How it works

Three simple steps to your moment of calm.

1

Choose your shape

Pick a breathing shape that feels right — box, circle, triangle, or more. Each one guides a different breathing rhythm.

2

Set the mood

Select your color mood and rhythm speed. Make the experience yours — calm, balanced, or deep.

3

Breathe

Follow the fluid visual guide. Inhale, hold, exhale, rest. Let the shape lead and your nervous system settle.
